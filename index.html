    ABOUT ME

    Building websites and applications is about solving problems, being creative, and being persistant until the project is finished; and it is a career that is a dream come true for me. In the Air Force, my job was to maintain and troubleshoot F-15 electronics systems. I enjoyed troubleshooting these systems; learning about how they work, finding problems, then fixing them. After getting out of the Air Force and trying out a few jobs like truck driving, and installing cable, I was missing that sense of accomplishment and purpose that I had in the service.
    Since starting on the path of software engineering, I have become immersed in all of the possibilities of things to build, and things to fix, and have found my calling.
    Aside from coding, I enjoy making music, and playing/ watching soccer.
